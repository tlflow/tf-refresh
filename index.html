Spending time as both designer and/or developer in many different settings gives me the "know-how" to work projects from start to finish.

So, Who is this Terence guy?

I'm a web developer and multimedia designer offering design services for both web and print mediums. Over the past decade, I've worked with a constant growing client base consisting of corporations, other web developers, design firms, non-profit organizations and retail businesses.

What He's All About

I like to think of myself as an intuitive, creative thinker and problem-solver. My passion is helping others achieve their goals using what I've leardevelopment and design and the web just happens to be my preferred medium. My business-like approach to creativity and design result in an array of media representation that is specifically meaningful and relevant to my clients and their consumer base. I spend a lot of time creating original designs and exploring new programming techniques.

Web Design and Development

Solid working knowledge with HTML5, XHTML, CSS standards compliant design; experience designing for integrated language and script platforms such as JavaScript, JQuery, and PHP.

Print and Brand Identities

Experience in nearly all print media such as collateral brand identity (logos, stationery, brochures, flyers), publication (magazine advertising, and publication design), and advertising.
